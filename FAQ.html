Frequently Asked Questions
What’s A Perfect Plate?
A Perfect Plate is a service that connects you and your family to a personal chef at a fraction of the cost. This service allows you the flexibility of having one or multiple meals cooked at your location of choice.
How it works?
It’s simple! Choose a plan, select your chef (or we can assign one) and choose your date and time. That’s it!!! You can now sit back and relax.
Will my chef provide the ingredients
The beauty of A Perfect Plate is that your chef will use what you currently have in your pantry, your kitchen, and spice rack to prepare the meal. You are paying simply for their service and time. So continue your grocery shopping as normal and keep your same pots and pans.* Our chefs will whip up a delicious meal for you and create and unforgettable experience. If you want to try something new, our chef’s will be happy to oblige. They may recommend additional ingredients that you may need to purchase for your experience.
Are there any special items I need in order to use A Perfect Plate?
No. Your chef knows their way around a kitchen and provided the opportunity, yours as well. There are a few basic requirements that you will need. Please see our Client Kitchen Code for more details.
Are there chefs that can prepare meals for those with allergies, special dietary restrictions or have particular taste and experience requirements?
Yes. Your chef is highly trained and well tested to deliver quality meals to you and your guests with all needs in mind. Just them let know.

*Please see the Client Kitchen Code that provides details of basic needs for your kitchen.
